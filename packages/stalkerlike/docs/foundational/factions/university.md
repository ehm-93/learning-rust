# University Faction

## Overview
**Population**: 300 (6% of colony)  
**Territory**: Levels 0-2km (within Corporate zone)  
**Power**: 10 MW (1 voltaic bank, Charter-protected)  
**Mission**: Build FTL drive to escape (152 years of failure)  
**Status**: Gilded cage within Corporate territory

## History

### Original Charter
Established with special autonomy to pursue scientific research. Charter guarantees:
- Independent voltaic bank (10 MW)
- Academic freedom (theoretical)
- Protected status (on paper)
- Self-governance (illusory)

### The Gamma Ray Discovery (Year 13)
University arrays detected Earth's destruction, providing independent confirmation of the nuclear exchange. This discovery triggered D+13 when combined with evidence of Corporate's cover-up.

### The Archivist Incident (Year 13)
University archivist (child of disappeared Board member) discovered:
- 73 deleted transmissions 
- Matched gamma ray timing exactly
- Corporate threatened them with parent's fate
- Fled to deep tunnels, never returned
- Set precedent for University resistance

### 152 Years of Failure
Attempting to recreate FTL technology:
- Can't systematize physics violations
- C-7 breaks causality itself
- "One breakthrough away" (said for 150 years)
- Doomed quest that justifies existence

## Internal Politics

### The Loyalists (30%)
- Report colleagues to Corporate
- Believe in "working within system"
- Receive preferential treatment
- Enable Corporate control

### The Collaborators (50%)
- Self-censor to survive
- Focus on "pure" research
- Avoid political topics
- Terrified of purges

### The Dissidents (20%)
- Secret Union sympathizers
- Share intelligence carefully
- Plan for eventual defection
- Risk death if discovered

## Corporate Control

### Embedded Security
- 20 Corporate officers on campus
- Constant surveillance
- Monitor all research
- Report "suspicious" behavior

### Regular Purges
- Every decade: 5-15 arrested
- Charged with "Union sympathy"
- Disappear without trial
- Keeps rest terrorized

### Resource Control
- Food from Corporate
- Equipment from Corporate
- Families live in Corporate zones
- Total dependency despite "autonomy"

## The Tragedy

300 brilliant minds trapped in elaborate prison, forced to chase impossible dream while documenting their own extinction. They know the truth about everything - Earth's death, Corporate's crimes, Union's violence, C-7's mysteries - but can't act on any of it.

Every decade, their bravest disappear. Every year, their hope dims. Every day, they pretend progress on FTL while watching the colony die around them.

They are historians of apocalypse, scientists of the impossible, prisoners in ivory tower. When the reactor fails, they'll finally have to choose: collaboration, revolution, or death.

The smartest people in the colony, and the most powerless. Their Charter protects them and enslaves them. Their knowledge empowers them and paralyzes them.

In the end, they might be the ones to save everyone - or document exactly how everyone died.
