# Ross 154a - Timeline

## Pre-Isolation Era
**Year 0-3**: FTL economy collapse, waiting for Earth contact

## The Secret (Year 3)
**Day 287**: 
- 14:37 UTC: First automated transmission about nuclear exchange
- 14:37-20:37 UTC: 73 transmissions over 6 hours
- 20:37 UTC: Final transmission cuts mid-word
- Board vote 6-3 to suppress information

**Day 288**: Emergency board session, decision to maintain operations

## The Purge (Year 4)
**Days 4-19**: Three dissenting board members disappear
- Families vanish or claim ignorance
- Communications staff "transferred"
- All 73 transmissions deleted from archives
- Three board seats filled within month

## The Revelation (Year 13)
**Day 287**: 
- University detects ~7,000 gamma signatures from Sol
- Confirms nuclear detonations from Year 3
- Archivist discovers 73 deletions matching gamma date
- Archivist flees to deep tunnels after Corporate threats

## D+13 (Years 13-15)
**35,000 dead in 2 years**
- Riots and purges: ~5,000 dead
- Surface Exodus: 15,000 flee, all dead within 2 years
- The Descent: 3,000 flee deep, 2,500 die/transform, 500 survive
- Corporate Siege: 3,000 Union members sealed and starved
- Population: 50,000 → 13,000

## Early Reconstruction (Years 15-47)
- Slow population decline begins
- Factions solidify: Corporate, Union, emerging Frontier
- Crystalline Faith forms from 500 Descent survivors
- Cold tensions, no major violence

## The Voltaic Strike (Year 47)
**Month 3**: 
- Corporate seals Sector D-7, 180 dead
- Union votes 62% for "surgical strike"
- Radiation breach kills 1,200 total
- Both sides lie about death toll

## The Retaliation Years (47-55)
- Systematic assassination campaign
- All three saboteurs killed
- ~30 Union members assassinated
- Sectors E-2/E-3/E-4 sealed permanently

## The Cold Civil War (Years 55-165)
**110 years of stalemate**
- All factions exhausted, unable to advance without overextending
- Proxy warfare through Frontier
- ~200 Corporate defectors (~80 succeed)
- Whisper cults emerge and spread chaos
- Zero meaningful territorial gains

## Population Decline (Years 15-165)
- 13,000 → 5,000 survivors
- 8,000 transformed into Touched
- ~0.5% annual decline rate
- C-7 infertility increases

## Infrastructure Decay
- Year 0: 24 reactors (4 GW capacity)
- Year 50: 5 reactors operational
- Year 100: 2 reactors operational
- Year 165: 1 reactor at 30% capacity
- 96% of original power capacity lost

## Present Day (Year 165)
- 5,000 survivors in five factions
- Reactor could fail any day
- Frontier largest faction but fractured
- ~100 Whisper cultists creating chaos
- Three board members still unaccounted for
- No contact with Earth for 162 years
